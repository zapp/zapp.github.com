Video and board games are a passion of mine - I love to play them with friends or enjoy them myself on cold winter nights.

When i started to design little board game prototypes myself and devoured the available literature on the topic, i realized that game development is in fact different from playing, but something just as fullfiling. And that i wanted to do it!

That was when i decided to dive deeper into game design and found advice and company in tutors and fellow students at my university in Berlin.

Currently, I'm in the final phase of my studies, doing the design on a larger student project. It is called Spectaculum - A Gipsy Adventure and you can find more about it in the projects section of my website.

Nice of you dropping by.
